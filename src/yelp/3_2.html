Specialties

More than 70,000 people a year visit our Emergency Center, making it the fifth busiest in Massachusetts. Our $20 million, 41-bay Emergency Center is a model with an average triage time of only 14 minutes, it is also one of the most efficient. We have a clinical affiliation with Beth Israel Deaconess Medical Center, greatly strengthening our scope and quality of services. In an affiliation with the Floating Hospital for Children at Tufts Medical Center, pediatricians from Floating Hospital are on-site 24/7 at Lawrence General in our family-friendly Pediatric Center. Our $5 million Imaging Center, includes the only "Ambient Experience" MRI system in MA. Services include a new 4-suite Sleep Center, Diabetes & Nutrition Education Center and Minimally Invasive Thoracic Surgery (MITS) Clinic. Our Cardiac Cath Lab is one of the busiest and most efficient in the State, with a "door to balloon" time averaging under 65 minutes, while the gold standard for these cardiac procedures is 90 minutes.

History

Established in 1875.

In 1875, a group of concerned women who were active in Lawrence's churches and who were the wives of the city's prominent businessmen established the Ladies' Union Charitable Society. Their objective was to improve the lives of the city's workers through good works and social assistance. Their initial efforts centered on establishing a "day nursery" to care for sick children whose working mothers could not take care of them during the day.
