My experience with Highland was not as a patient, but instead, a support for a loved one who needed surgery. I am a Buffalo native and our hospitals overall, are over crowded and disorganized, so dealing with Highland was such a nice change of pace. I felt the facilities were well maintained and that parking was hassle-free, which are huge perks I'm interested in when being at such a stressful place! All of the staff was friendly. I did stay overnight and the nurses did their jobs efficiently, and really tried to let the person I was there for get some real sleep (completely unheard of in any hospital) although I'm sure this experience will vary based on your physical needs! Having spent the whole day around the halls/lobby/surgery registration and prep rooms, I can say that I felt things were generally organized with staff and they do wonderfully with having ample (and comfortable!) waiting areas in the lobby, monitors to track the status of your loved one, and outlets. If you ever needed to step away from the waiting areas, you return your buzzer to the front desk and they take your number to call you if anything changed or happened with the person having a procedure done, which I was very very pleased with and anxious to have as an option. I was most impressed with the food options for those not in a patient capacity. Never have I ever been in a hospital that offered such an array of items. In the cafeteria, they have Grab and Go options, multiple coolers, fresh coffee, fresh fruits, puddings, a breakfast buffet, etc. among other things and they even had a made-to-order stove top area where I was able to get a breakfast sandwich made not of fake, rubbery typical hospital food, but real eggs! It was really delicious and such a nice touch. They also have a "Cafe" type spot in another area of the hospital where you can order off of a menu as one would in a cafe style restaurant- I tried this for lunch and was pleased once more with the range of options and the overall quality. Their pharmacy was a great asset to have for picking up the scripts given instead of having to worry about that upon your return home. As we were out of towners, this was incredibly helpful and also cured the issue of it being a weekend, when the pharmacy we would have needed otherwise is unfortunately closed.
