I was having severe back and abdominal pain. I went to emergency here and was taken in immediately. They did a CT and diagnosed Kidney Stones. I was sent on my way in just a couple of hours after coming up with a plan for for pain management. The doctor was great and my nurse, Nicole, was incredible. The results for all of my tests came back quickly. The staff was friendly and fast, which I needed at the time! Thank you!!!

I had been seen Sunday evening and Tuesday evening at their emergency room. The wait times were ridiculous but that comes with the nature of an ER, I guess. The experience itself was ok. I am a perfectly healthy 22 year old girl with EXCELLENT veins. I have never had anyone have to stick me more than once when putting in an IV. I was diagnosed with a ovarian cyst. The second time they had said the cyst grew and that is why I was in so much pain. I was referred to see an OBGYN. The OBGYN can't see me until May 23rd unless someone can send them something showing it is more urgent. I tried calling the ER to speak to someone and was put on hold for 30 minutes just for someone name "Mary Peters" to cut me off while I'm explaining my situation, and be extremely rude to me. Coming from someone in the military and has seen many doctors. Do NOT go here. You have a better chance of feeling better by watching a doctors TV show than to go here.

This is such a shame of a hospital in certain respects. I will let all judge for themselves but wow....speechless. Too bad. Administration should be wiped out and replaced. A 21 year ild out of college can do a better job probably in managing and in professionalism. Again...my opinion. Go ahead...try it for yourself. Be observant ..just..be observant.

Wow, this is the WORST hospital I've ever been too. I waited in the ER for about an hour, and then when I was taken to the back I waited another hour for the physician to come in for 2 minutes and diagnose me without even examining me, & then referring me to Guthrie on post and they have a two month wait over there smh. THE DRIVE MAY BE FAR BUT I WILL GO TO CARTHAGE AREA HOSPITAL, BECAUSE THEY ARE AWESOME!

Worst hospital I have ever been too 5 month old daughter checked in to ER at 915pm and its 1130pm and everyone is walking straight back an ahe never got her vitals took which I though was standard for all infants coming too the ER.

They may or may not help you out but either way they will send you 50 bills even if you paid. There are a couple good staff members, but most won't even give you the time of day. This hospital is all about the bottom line, no care for individuals whatsoever.

After arriving for my first appointment here I stood for a good 20 minutes at the registration desk while the two receptionists made no acknowledgement that I was there. I stepped right up to the counter seeing as waiting at the line was not helping, and again they would not even look at me or say "We'll be with you in a moment". After an extremely uncomfortable amount of time with no response from either of the women at the desk, Ashley from registration took it upon herself to assist me. She directed me where I needed to go and I thanked her for her help. I appreciate that she saw the situation unfolding and stopped it. At this point there were a few people behind me in line wondering why no one was being helped. If you work at registration you are the first face people see when entering the facility, they should be respectful and knowledgeable about their jobs not lacking customer service skills. If it is a shift change, just let people know to take a seat and you'll be with them as soon as you can. I went to my appointment upon which the office was closed and a tiny sign saying due to in climate weather they closed early. Not even a phone call or voicemail was made to let patients know they would be unable to see us today? That would be common courtesy though... No phone number for the office was listed anywhere on the door. I returned to registration and let her know I appreciated her, but the office was closed. (By the way the other people were still standing in line waiting for the receptionists to see them at this point). I said it was really unprofessional how things were being run and asked for a number of a manager to call. They gave me the HR department. The woman called me back, mentioned she got my voicemail and then pawned me off completely. "Oh that isn't our office even though it's in our building..." So the people that work at your reception desk have nothing to do with you? She then told me she would let their manager know, but never asked me anything about what had happened. I was really disappointment about everything that happened during my experience at this location. Although my review will not change anything, I do hope that the people that read it will know to go somewhere else when seeking medical treatment. There are plenty of other places in the area to go for appointments, save yourself the headache of unprofessional, rude employees.

We tried multiple places for a pediatrician and finally ended up here. We see Dr. Jain who is extremely thorough, personable and so friendly. He's been great with my kids and he's my doctor as well. The wait times are never long once we arrive (maybe 15 minutes from when we arrive to see the doctor) and we are able to make an appointment quickly too. I highly recommend coming here if you are looking for a pediatrician or family doctor!
