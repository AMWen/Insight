My room was always kept clean and I was really, really surprisingly impressed with the choices on the menu and how good the food was! I had initially thought that I would go into Manhattan and use a more well known hospital. I'm very happy that instead I chose to go to Dr Germano and had my surgery at South Nassau. Specialties

For every accident and illness, for every health challenge you face, and for support in helping you stay as healthy as possible, South Nassau is dedicated to serving you. We're your neighbors here on the South Shore.

We have an impressive range of services both at our hospital and at our many locations in the communities we serve. But our clearest commitment comes in the form of people -- the staff and physicians who have made your health care their life's work.

And so our mission is clear: to bring you a full range of high-quality health care right here, close to your work, home, family and friends. Connect with us today.

History

Established in 1928.

At South Nassau Communities Hospital, we are at the center of a health care renaissance: one that's squarely focused on the health and well-being of you and your family. Providing superior, quality patient care is at the center of all we do here at South Nassau. We are dedicated to making your time with us as comfortable as possible, providing you with the state-of-the-art health services you need, together with the compassionate care you deserve. We believe that our employees and physicians are our most valued resources. Call us today for more information.
