Mount Auburn Hospital is a Harvard-affiliated regional teaching hospital serving the greater metropolitan Boston-Cambridge area. Founded in 1886, the hospital is committed to delivering clinical excellence with true compassion.

Mount Auburn hosts rotating residents and fellows covering medical and surgical specialties. It also provides care at over 40 outpatient offices through Mount Auburn Professional Services, the hospital's employed multi-specialty physician group.
