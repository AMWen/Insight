Specialties

The Flushing Hospital Medical Center, also known as FHMC, is 290-plus-bed, nonprofit teaching hospital. Founded in 1884, it operates a full-service emergency department that consists of adult and pediatric units. The center performs medical procedures in the areas of immunology, neurology and neurology. The Flushing Hospital Medical Center offers nursing and pastoral care services. The center provides pain management and oncology solutions. It offers physical, occupational and speech therapies. The Flushing Hospital Medical Center accepts several insurance plans and policies. The center also provides treatment for various rheumatological disorders, such as osteoarthritis and osteoporosis. It features a 25-plus-bed acute inpatient care unit.
