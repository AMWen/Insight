I have to say, my doctor there was quite good, but the hospital as a whole is questionable. First, I had a surgery scheduled, and they neglected to submit the request to my insurance company until too late, so it had not been approved by the time the procedure came around. They tried to get me to sign something that said I would be liable for all charges if the insurance did not approve it, which would've been very irresponsible to do, so I had to reschedule the surgery for a month later, thus losing out on income from the jobs I had canceled already for the surgery that never happened, and for the actual surgery dates. The billing person was very nice and patient, but their system seems really disorganized. Second, on a separate occasion, I went in for a ER visit, and was (correctly) charged for an ER visit with a $250 co-pay. Then instead of recommending a follow-up with my PCP, they told me to come back in to the hospital for a follow-up. They never said this would be billed as another ER visit with another $250 co-pay. It seemed like this was a scheduled follow-up and would be billed as such (i.e. normal $25 co-pay and not a $250 ER co-pay). Imagine my surprise when we got two $250 bills in the mail, one for each visit! And I could've just as easily seen my PCP for the follow-up and only paid $25! I assume they wanted me to come back in so they could bill the insurance (and me) again and make more money. Even though this hospital has some amazing personnel and doctors that work there, it seems like they run on the model of "how can we make the most money" and not "how can we best serve our patients". I would try to look elsewhere as I'm sure there are doctors at other hospitals in the area who are just as good where you won't have to deal with billing nightmares.

Specialties

Holy Family Hospital is a 385-bed full-service, acute care hospital with two campuses located in Methuen and Haverhill, Massachusetts. Our Methuen location was established more than 60 years ago, in 1950 and in 2014 merged with Merrimack Valley Hospital. Both locations provide comprehensive inpatient, outpatient and 24/7 emergency services, including eICUs. With first class specialists, innovative treatment options and state-of-the-art technology, the hospital provides exceptional care throughout the greater Merrimack Valley and southern New Hampshire.

Specialties: Surgery (general, colorectal, neuro, vascular, ENT, gyn, urology, thoracic); Orthopedics; Maternity
