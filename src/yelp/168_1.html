I can't believe the negative reviews I've read about this hospital. I couldn't disagree any more with what was written. Once the decision was made they made sure to clear it with several doctors due to my medical history. All the doctors I met with were knowledgeable and well informed on my past. After my surgery I was sent to the second floor for post op. I was given my very own room. The nurses was very nice and made sure to check on me even when I didn't call for them. When I did call for them it didn't take more than a few moments to see what I needed. All nurses made sure to explain the "5 P's" to me. This is a system to ensure that all patients are comfortable and understand what they are entitled too as patients. The following day, both doctors (the cardiologist and the doctor who operated on me) came in and explained everything thoroughly to me. Once I spoke with them I was able to be discharged. Discharging from hospitals can usually take hours. I was out of there within the hour and was even given the option of getting a transporter to help discharge me which I happily accepted.
